# 🚀 Quick Start - Deploy Your Website in 2 Minutes

## Fastest Way: Netlify Drag & Drop

1. **Open:** https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. **Drag** your entire `pp` folder onto the page
3. **Done!** Your site is live at `https://random-name.netlify.app`

That's it! No signup needed for the first deploy.

---

## Want a Custom Name?

1. Sign up at [netlify.com](https://www.netlify.com) (free)
2. Drag your folder again
3. Go to Site Settings → Change site name
4. Your site: `https://your-custom-name.netlify.app`

---

## Other Options

- **Vercel:** https://vercel.com/new (drag & drop)
- **GitHub Pages:** Upload to GitHub → Settings → Pages

See `DEPLOYMENT.md` for detailed instructions.
